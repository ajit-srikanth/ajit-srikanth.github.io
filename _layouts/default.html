Supplimentary

I'd like to say I work on robot learning. My long-term research goal is to create agents which can learn from their (or some other embodiment's) prior experiences to perform tasks, and also being able to adapt to new tasks (and environments), without task-specific data.

Although a bit cliche, I wish to work towards where humans and robots coexist, with the robots assiting humans not only in dangerous tasks, but also boring (subjective) everyday tasks like cleaning, cooking, fetching, blah blah blah. Well, this will also enable them to serve as support systems for humans and also scale up accessibility to resources.
